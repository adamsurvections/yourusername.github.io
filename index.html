M&E Consultancy

Surveying that goes beyond reports —
insight you can act on.

Most surveyors compete on price and speed. Survections structures survey data for reuse, comparison, and long-term asset intelligence — the kind clients actually build strategies around.

  • M&E condition surveys
    Detailed inspection of mechanical and electrical systems — HVAC, electrical distribution, plumbing, fire. Full written reports produced to Tetra Tech and TfL audit standard.
  • Asset registers & lifecycle planning
    M&E asset schedules with condition ratings, remaining useful life, and projected replacement costs. Delivered in Excel, Power BI, or Kykloud.
  • PPM strategies & cost forecasting
    Forward maintenance cost models and planned preventative maintenance schedules — budget-aware, practically written, designed to be actioned.
  • Portfolio & multi-site surveys
    Proven high-volume delivery across council and infrastructure portfolios. Scalable with consistent grading and data structure across every site.
  • Outside IR35 · weekend & ad-hoc
    Available for contract assignments outside IR35, including weekend and ad-hoc delivery across West and Central London.
